About Us

Flora

    A legacy that began over two decades ago.


    As pioneers in our field in the beautiful city of eternal romance, Tezpur, we have spent the last 25+ years filling your lives with diverse flavors. It has been a journey of serving you better, happier, and sweeter, guided by the blessings of Lord Ganesha and your unwavering love and support.
    Through the years, our dedication remains unchanged—to delight you with mouthwatering dishes, from the bamboo shoots of the North-East to the aromatic spices of North India , all served in a warm and welcoming ambiance.
    For more than 25 glorious years, we have steadfastly upheld the same principle: we cherish those small, happy-go-lucky, nameless moments of joy—the laughter, the sharing, the memories you create with your loved ones here at Flora.
    We extend our deepest gratitude for your incredible support over the years and look forward to your continued love in the future.

Our Motive

    At our restaurant, we believe food is more than just a meal—it's an experience. We’re committed to serving delicious, freshly prepared dishes that bring comfort and joy. With a passion for flavor and hospitality, we aim to create a warm, welcoming space for everyone. Our goal is to celebrate taste, tradition, and togetherness in every bite.
